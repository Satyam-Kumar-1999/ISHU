For My Dearest Ishani

In Asansol, where the sun does rise,
A city blessed, where beauty lies,
Not in the streets or skies above,
But in the heart of the one I love.

Ishani, with grace, the ground she treads,
Turns mundane paths to golden threads,
For Asansol is worthy, true and bright,
Because she breathes its air, day and night.

Baskin Robbins, a simple delight,
Becomes divine with her every bite,
For in her hands, the flavors bloom,
And in her smile, the world's perfume.

No sight is grand, no touch so sweet,
Until Ishani's presence makes it complete,
She is like a goddess, pure and kind,
An angel on earth, in heart and mind.

Her scent, a whisper, I can't resist,
Her neck, a temptation, I can't desist,
Addicted am I, to all she gives,
For in her light, my soul truly lives.

Ishani, a pure soul, the finest I've seen,
In her reflection, the world turns serene,
Satyam Bharadwaj loves her, beyond all measure,
For she is his life, his greatest treasure.
